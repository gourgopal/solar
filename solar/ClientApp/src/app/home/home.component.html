SD Solar Energy

Solar Energy is a renewable source of energy. The main source of Solar Energy is the Sun, which is a free and abundant source of energy. The Solar Panel collects the rays of the sun during the day and converts it into energy, which could be stored or used.

As compared to 1970s, prices of Solar Panels have decreased drastically. Solar panel systems are a very good way for investment whether or not you intent to sell your home or stay because they last so long. Modern solar panels last at least 25 years and it is hard to know how long they will really last because the ones built in the 1990s are still producing power. Current modern panels are made to last even longer and they give freedom to do simple additions for growing the power production whenever needed and as per your budget.

It is easy to install solar panels. This can save much of your money by not paying the electricity bills. Most of the solar panels in India comes with 25 years of warranty so you can consider installing one of the eco-friendly devices and save the environment from harmful emissions. Using solar power systems can improve the environment.

Solar Panel systems can save the environment in many ways:

  1. Cutting down Carbon Emissions – Coal plants, vehicles, households emits carbon into the atmosphere creating pollution. Solar Power system can reduce all these emissions as it is the does not depend on burning fossil fuels.
  2. Reducing Water Usage – The nuclear and coal plants draw up to 300 litres of water for every kilowatt-hour of electricity produced. Solar technology does not use any!
  3. Improving Public Health – Solar energy does not create noise or release any toxic gases into the atmosphere, keeping the environment green and clean which means it improves life expectancy and reduces the mortality rate of surrounding communities.

Our homes, vehicles, and technologies require immense amounts of energy generated by oil, coal or hydro, which are non-renewable source i.e. available in limited whereas, oil and coal add to greenhouse gas build-up in the environment. However, that is not the case in Solar Energy. The Sunshine, however, is not disappearing anytime soon.

Solar energy is a clean, affordable, and sustainable way to generate electricity. It is a way to reduce our reliance on fossil fuels in favour of the most abundant source of energy available - the sun. The cumulative effect of homeowners and businesses adopting solar technology across the nation is what really makes a lasting impact. That is why your decision to go solar can make such a positive difference.
